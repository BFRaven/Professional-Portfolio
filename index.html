About me

With a focus in language translation, interpreting and learning, I am seeking to restructure communication barriers around the world through web and mobile application development. I aim to develop technology that is accessible, user-friendly, and comprehensive to users globally.

Through the University of Minnesota, I have been trained and certified in full-stack programming. In addition, I have also been formally trained as a translator and interpreter through Century college in St. Paul Minnesota. I am fluent in both the English and Spanish languages and working proficiency in the German language. In addition, I have extensive customer service experience in the restaurant, retail and mobile technology fields.

ForeignExchange

Foreign Exchange

This application utilizes html, css, bootstrap, javascript, jquery, Node.js, sequelize, mysql, handlebars.js, RESTful, and Microsoft's Translation API.

The end result is an application that allows users to critique the machine translation given by Microsoft, vote on which translations are most accurate, and search for terms in many language so that people can learn the proper way of speaking in another language.

Create-A-Planet

You will be given a random number at the start of the universe.

Below, are the four elements needed to create a planet to add to the universe. By clicking on an element you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You create a planet by matching your total score to the random number, if your total score goes above the random number, the planet will not form.

The value of each element is hidden from you until you click on it.

At the start of each universe, the values of each element will change.
